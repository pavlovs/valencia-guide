How Valencia Works
  • Almuerzo (9:30-11:30am): Bocadillo + beer + cremaet. Try Kiosco La Pergola or La Cantina de Ruzafa.
  • Lunch: 1:30-4pm, the main meal. Paella goes here.
  • Dinner: Not before 9pm. Seriously.
  • Bars: Nobody before 11pm.
  • Coffee: Never pay more than 2 EUR.
  • Avoid: Placa de la Reina, Cavallers strip, Malvarrosa beach restaurants, photo menus.
Before You Go

Book NOW:

  • Casa Carmela — 1-2 weeks ahead, phone only
  • Nozomi — 3-5 days, book the downstairs room
  • Saiti — 2-3 days, website
  • Flama — 2-3 days, Instagram or phone
  • Forastera — phone, a few days

Transport: Cabify (not Uber). Bolt also works. Valenbisi bike share for beach. City is walkable.

Paella rules: Lunch only, NEVER dinner. Never in El Carmen tourist zone. Only wood fire. Book + pre-order rice.

Sunday SOS

Open on Sunday: La Pilareta, Ostras Pedrin, Cafe de las Horas, Horno San Bartolome, Borgia Wine Bar, Tinto Fino Ultramarino, The Green Book, Canalla Bistro, La Cooperativa del Mar, all rooftops, Horchateria Daniel, Gelatica.

Special May 17: Salon Vins Naturals (10-18h at La Rambleta).
